MY ACCOUNT

Subscriptions

You can view and manage your current subscription plan under Subscriptions page.

What you can do?

  • View your current plan and its status.
  • Switch between Monthly or Annual billings.
  • Manage or purchase Add-Ons.
  • Cancel subscription.
  • Manage payment method information.
  1. 1

    Go to Account and click on Subscriptions.

  2. 2

    In Current Plan section, you can see the name and status of your plan.

  3. 3

    Below it, you can check if your plan is on Monthly or Annual billing.

  4. 4

    If you wish to upgrade your current plan, click on "Upgrade Plan" button.

  5. 5

    If you wish to cancel your subscription, click on "Cancel Subscription" link and follow the instructions given.

  6. 6

    Under Payment Method section, you can see the payment method you have used to pay for your subscription. You can also add a new payment method for easier future payments.
